About

TaskFlow

TaskFlow is a project inspired by my need for better organization in life. I wanted a tool to track daily tasks, goals, and even manage my budget—all in one place. Balancing work, personal goals like going to the gym, and finances was a challenge, and TaskFlow became the solution. While it started as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, it quickly grew into something more personal. TaskFlow reflects both my technical growth and my journey to stay on top of life's demands. Explore TaskFlow and see how it can help organize your tasks, habits and finances!
